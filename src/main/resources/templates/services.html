Verification

Things you'll need ...
  • Document for identification (Id, Driver License or Passport)
  • Document that confirms the address in the identification document you submit(yes they must match).
  • Working computer camera to take a photo of you holding the identification document...

Once we confirm that you are really you , hahah, your account is going to be verified and you get:
  • 5000$ monthly transactions limit.
  • Option to order GecaPay Debit Card.
  • Option to make withdrawl directly to your bank account(I don't really know you you will need this if you have a GecaPay Card, but still you have it...).
  • Option GecaPay Virtual Debit Card to be created for online shopping until your Plastic GecaPay Debit Card arrive on your address.
